An introduction to an RPG based mobile game. We choose a mobile platform to implement the game since it's the most accessible and friendly to use by people all around the world.


Crimson Saga utilizes the following software:

  1. Unity & unreal engine servers, Blender, Corel, Art studios, and Photoshop for most of the arts and sceneries.

About The Game

Crimson Saga is a P2E RPG game where players can purchase items, character skins, armory, and defenses using REALM native in game tokens. In-game Realm will Also have burning mechanisms via upgrading skills, spells, weapons, and defenses. Players will need to grind or purchase rare weapon or armor to help the player have an advantage in PVP in the adventure mode chapters as they progress throughout the game.

The game will always be evolving with new content and lore. The storyline starts with the webcomic and branch out eventually to our vision of the metaverse. Where users can create, sale their art and enjoy themselves in a variety of different formats and graphics to their pleasure. Most of the characters will have their own race, traits and specialties within the Crimson Saga game.
