An NFT market in the crypto space which uses a peer-to-peer system that can enable anyone anywhere to send and receive payments in the form of a digital art. The Galileo Market. It is a unique digital venue that will let people mint, sell, and trade NFTs using different blockchains. Not only can they mint in Polygon but also in Solana or Ethereum.

Advice will be given to educate them about the many competitors and NFT markets. As a result, users can choose between other NFTs markets; Rarelia.com, Solanart.io, and Opensea.io which allows transaction within their respective blockchain or to use our Multi Chain NFT Market.

The following wallets will be integrated into the Galileo Market, so users have a variety of possibilities to connect to use our services: Metamask, Phantom, Coinbase, Bitski, Venly, WalletConnect and Nexo wallet etc.


The following advantages can be obtained in using our Galileo Market:

  1. Services offered for artists to outsource help of any kind whether it’s music, art, 3D, PFP, and complete collections creations. Only 5% of the proceedings will be taken out of each sale and it will go towards the treasury which will directly benefit investors and early adopters with in the KatsCo ecosystem in the form of staking other benefits.

  2. The minting of NFTs will have a gas fee according to each of their blockchain fees respectively. One good thing about our gas fee is that we will try to minimize it with discounts by using the TENGEN token and by using other smart protocols which will set us apart from other NFT markets.

  3. The market will try its best to prioritize freedom without further restrictions to the best of our abilities so artist can express themselves as much as they want.

  4. Whenever NFT of high value are exported out of the gallery from our control, a 2F authentication will be required to safeguard the assets of our users.

  5. NFTs from the gallery will be deleted that we deemed to be copied or are not authorized for distribution by the original creators or artist.

  6. Users are allowed to trade NFTs among themselves without the need of selling which is a unique trait we will have.

  7. Some transactions are deemed to be taxable, yet it’s an even trade without the monetary value, it’s exempted in a decentralized way. Since it’s an even trade, no sale made which will benefit the users for countries that hinder them via taxation.

What are the goals of Galileo Market?

To give ease of access to the end users and have transparency of their creations as far as market choices and while giving them choices for gas fees to better allocate their NFT collections.
